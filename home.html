Curriculum

On this page, you'll find all instructional materials developed and curated by the Antiracist Curriculum Project team, hosted by PathStone. Our aim is empowering students, educators, and communities with instructional resources about their local history of racism and civil rights.Ultimately, we work to cultivate more informed and engaged community members to build a more just and equitable society.
